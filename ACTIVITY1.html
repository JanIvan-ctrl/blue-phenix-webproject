Motors

Kawasaki Motors, a subsidiary of Kawasaki Heavy Industries, is a Japanese mobility manufacturer known for producing motorcycles, all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), utility vehicles,
watercraft (including the iconic "Jet Ski"), and general-purpose gasoline engines.Kawasaki's roots trace back to 1878 when Shozo Kawasaki established the Kawasaki Tsukiji Shipyard in Tokyo.

Top Sales

The Kawasaki Ninja ZX-10R is a motorcycle in the Ninja sport bike series from the Japanese manufacturer Kawasaki, the successor to the Ninja ZX-9R. It was originally released in 2004 and has been updated and revised throughout the years. It combines an ultra-narrow chassis, low weight, and radial brakes.
